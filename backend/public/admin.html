How It Works

A clean view of the scheduling flow, plus a quick way to preview the live customer experience without leaving the admin console.

Customer Experience Open Live Scheduler Preview the public booking page in a new tab. Launch Preview
1

Connect Your Calendars

Add each personal or business calendar you want included in your availability. The system supports up to 7 connected calendars.

2

Booking Calendar Availability

Customer-facing time slots come from the calendar you mark as the booking destination, so the public schedule reflects the consultation calendar you actually use.

3

Live Sync

As you create or edit events in Google Calendar, your booking availability updates automatically so customers always see a current schedule.

4

Booking Flow

Customers choose from your live availability, submit their request, and the confirmed consultation is posted to the calendar you marked as the booking destination.

Keep this admin page open while testing. The scheduler opens in a separate tab so you can verify the customer experience without losing your current configuration view.
